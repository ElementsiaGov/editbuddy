Frequently Asked Questions

What services does EditBuddy offer?

EditBuddy specializes in website development, graphic design, and event branding. We offer customized solutions to help your business or event stand out with high-quality designs and fully responsive websites.

How long does it take to create a website or design?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A basic website design typically takes 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features may take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline after discussing your project.

What information do you need to start a project?

To start your project, we need details about your business or event, the type of design or website you're looking for, and any specific elements you'd like to include (such as logos, branding guidelines, or color schemes). We’ll guide you through the process to make sure we capture your vision.

Do you provide support after the project is completed?

Yes, we provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure that your website or design continues to perform optimally. We also offer additional services like updates, redesigns, and assistance with new features.

What is the cost of your services?

Our pricing varies based on the scope of the project. Website designs start at R1250, and branding or graphic design services are priced based on the complexity and number of deliverables. We’ll provide a custom quote after reviewing your needs.
