How to Proceed with Your Payment

Already Placed an Order?

If you have already placed an order, follow these steps:

  • 1. Check your email for the invoice we sent.
  • 2. Open the invoice and review the details to confirm everything is correct.
  • 3. Choose your preferred payment method (Credit Card, PayPal, EFT).
  • 4. Complete your payment within the invoice's due date to avoid delays.
  • 5. Contact our support team if you have any questions.
View Invoices

Looking to Order Something?

If you’re looking to place an order for a website or design, here’s what you need to do:

  • 1. Browse our services and choose the one that fits your needs.
  • 2. Select a financing option that works for you (e.g., Pay After Launch, Split Payments).
  • 3. Fill out the order form with all the necessary details.
  • 4. Submit the form and we’ll review your request.
  • 5. Once your order is approved, we’ll send you a confirmation email with next steps.
